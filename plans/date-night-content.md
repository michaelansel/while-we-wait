# "Date Night" — Content Plan for While We Wait

## 1. Game metadata

- **id (slug):** `datenight`
- **Display name:** Date Night
- **Emoji icon:** 🥂
- **Accent color (hue):** `#FF5D8F` — warm rosé-pink; sits nicely alongside the existing mint `#3DDC97` and sky `#4CC9F0`, and reads "date" without being saccharine. (Backup option if too pink against the palette: coral `#FF7059`.)
- **energy:** `calm`
- **Audience note:** couples of any stage — first dates to long-married. Every card works either way. PG-13 max, warm > raunchy.

## 2. Deck structure

**One shuffled deck of mixed prompts + a small bonus "Would You Rather" set mixed in** (using the existing `kind:'choice'` card format inside the same deck if the engine supports mixed kinds; otherwise keep the choice cards as a natural cluster at the end and shuffle at runtime).

Why mixed rather than named categories: the magic of a conversation deck is the surprise of not knowing if the next card is silly or deep — formal categories invite over-thinking ("let's do the deep ones") and make short waits feel like homework. The variety is built into the shuffle. Internally the ~52 prompt cards balance out roughly as:

- ~10 light icebreakers / silly
- ~9 playful & flirty (tasteful)
- ~10 "us" — memories & how-we-met
- ~9 deep / reflective
- ~7 gratitude & appreciation
- ~7 future dreams & plans
- +10 Would You Rather (choice format)

## 3. How to play

> Read the card out loud. One of you answers first — then the other (no "same!" allowed, give your own answer). Follow-up questions are encouraged, phones are not.

## 4. The cards (`{e, k, p}` format)

### Light icebreakers / silly

1. e: 🍕 | k: "What food could you eat every day for a year?" | p: "Follow-up: which food should be banned from this table forever?"
2. e: 🦸 | k: "What's your most useless talent?" | p: "Demonstrations at the table are encouraged (within reason)."
3. e: 🎤 | k: "What song do you secretly know every word to?" | p: "Bonus: hum four seconds of it right now."
4. e: 🛒 | k: "What's the weirdest thing you've ever bought at 11pm?" | p: "Online shopping confessions count double."
5. e: 🐙 | k: "If you were an animal, which one — and which one am I?" | p: "You have to explain your reasoning. Flattery optional."
6. e: 🕵️ | k: "What's a hill you will absolutely die on?" | p: "The pettier, the better. Defend it like a lawyer."
7. e: 👻 | k: "What's your most irrational fear?" | p: "Follow-up: has it ever actually happened?"
8. e: 🍿 | k: "What movie can you quote way too much of?" | p: "Do the quote. Commit to the voice."
9. e: 🧦 | k: "What's the most 'you' thing you did this week?" | p: "The other person gets to confirm or overrule."
10. e: 🛸 | k: "You get one totally impractical superpower. What is it?" | p: "No flying, no invisibility — pick something weird."

### Playful & flirty (tasteful)

11. e: 😏 | k: "What was your first impression of me?" | p: "Be honest. Then say what changed — or what turned out true."
12. e: 💘 | k: "When did you first realize you liked me?" | p: "The exact moment, if you can. Details required."
13. e: 🔥 | k: "What do I do that you find weirdly attractive?" | p: "The weirder and more specific, the better."
14. e: 💃 | k: "Describe our first date from your side of the table." | p: "New couple? Describe THIS date so far instead."
15. e: 😉 | k: "What's your favorite compliment you've ever received?" | p: "Now give the other person one just as good."
16. e: 💋 | k: "What's your idea of a perfect kiss moment?" | p: "Rain? Doorstep? Middle of a sentence? Take notes."
17. e: 🎯 | k: "What little thing do I do that always makes you smile?" | p: "Bonus points if I didn't know I was doing it."
18. e: 🕺 | k: "If we had a couple's theme song, what would it be?" | p: "You each pick one, then negotiate. This is binding."
19. e: 🌹 | k: "What's the most romantic thing anyone's ever done for you?" | p: "The bar is now set. No pressure."

### "Us" — memories & how-we-met

20. e: 📸 | k: "What's a moment with me you wish you had on video?" | p: "Reenactments welcome."
21. e: 😂 | k: "What's the hardest we've ever laughed together?" | p: "Warning: retelling it may cause a repeat incident."
22. e: 🗺️ | k: "What's your favorite place we've been together?" | p: "Could be Paris. Could be a parking lot at 2am."
23. e: 🎁 | k: "What's the best gift you've ever gotten from anyone?" | p: "Follow-up: what made it perfect — the thing, or the person?"
24. e: 🍝 | k: "What's our most 'us' meal — the one that tastes like this relationship?" | p: "New couple? What meal do you predict will become 'yours'?"
25. e: ⏪ | k: "If you could relive one day of your life, which one?" | p: "Relive as-is — no changing anything. Just enjoying it again."
26. e: 🤝 | k: "What's something we survived together that made us stronger?" | p: "IKEA furniture counts."
27. e: 🧩 | k: "What did you learn about me that surprised you most?" | p: "Good surprises and 'huh, okay' surprises both count."
28. e: 🎪 | k: "What's the most spontaneous thing we've ever done?" | p: "Follow-up: should we do something like that again soon?"
29. e: 🏆 | k: "What's your proudest 'we did that' moment as a team?" | p: "Big or small — pulling off a move counts as much as a wedding."

### Deep / reflective

30. e: 🌊 | k: "What's something you've changed your mind about in the last few years?" | p: "Big or small — from politics to pineapple on pizza."
31. e: 🧭 | k: "What did you want to be when you were 8?" | p: "Follow-up: what would 8-year-old you think of your life now?"
32. e: 🎭 | k: "What's something people assume about you that isn't true?" | p: "Follow-up: do you ever play along with it?"
33. e: 🌱 | k: "What are you quietly working on becoming?" | p: "Not a job title — a kind of person."
34. e: 🕰️ | k: "What do you miss about being a kid?" | p: "Follow-up: which part could we actually bring back?"
35. e: 🎓 | k: "What's the best advice anyone ever gave you?" | p: "Follow-up: did you take it right away, or the hard way?"
36. e: 🌙 | k: "What keeps you up at night lately — good or bad?" | p: "Listen first. Fix later, only if asked."
37. e: 🪞 | k: "What's something you're better at than you give yourself credit for?" | p: "If they undersell it, the other person corrects the record."
38. e: 🕊️ | k: "What's something you've forgiven — or are still working on forgiving?" | p: "Yourself counts. Skip is allowed, but try."

### Gratitude & appreciation

39. e: 🙏 | k: "What's something I did recently that meant more than I probably realized?" | p: "Little things count the most here."
40. e: 💎 | k: "What quality of mine do you hope never changes?" | p: "Then swap. No deflecting with a joke — okay, one joke."
41. e: ☀️ | k: "What was the best part of your week?" | p: "Follow-up: what was the hardest part? Both deserve airtime."
42. e: 🫶 | k: "How do you most feel loved — words, time, help, gifts, or touch?" | p: "Follow-up: which one do you give the most? Are they the same?"
43. e: 🌟 | k: "Who's someone who shaped who you are — that I've never met (or barely know)?" | p: "Tell me their story in one minute."
44. e: 🏡 | k: "What makes somewhere feel like home to you?" | p: "A smell, a sound, a person, a chair?"
45. e: 🍀 | k: "What's something ordinary in your life you'd genuinely miss if it vanished?" | p: "Morning coffee, a commute podcast, this restaurant?"

### Future dreams & plans

46. e: ✈️ | k: "Where in the world do you most want to go together?" | p: "Dream big first — then name a trip we could actually do this year."
47. e: 🔮 | k: "Where do you hope we are five years from now?" | p: "Not just geography — what does a regular Tuesday look like?"
48. e: 🎨 | k: "What's something you've always wanted to learn?" | p: "Follow-up: what's actually stopping you? (Be honest.)"
49. e: 🏝️ | k: "Describe your perfect lazy day together." | p: "Hour by hour. Snacks must be specified."
50. e: 💰 | k: "We just won the lottery. What's the first completely frivolous thing we buy?" | p: "One each. No investing allowed for the first purchase."
51. e: 📅 | k: "What's one tiny tradition we should start?" | p: "Weekly, monthly, or 'every time it rains.' Pick one and actually start it."
52. e: 🚀 | k: "What's on your bucket list that I might not know about?" | p: "Follow-up: which one could we knock out together first?"

## Notes for implementation (content-side, not code)

- Tone matched to the app: warm, a little cheeky, second-person, em-dash asides — same voice as the kids' decks but grown up.
- Every couple-history card has an escape hatch for new couples (see #14, #24) or is phrased to work for any relationship length.
- The `p` line alternates roles deliberately: sometimes a follow-up question, sometimes a playful rule ("no 'same!' allowed" energy), sometimes flavor — mirroring how the kids' decks use it.
- Skips should always be allowed; card #38 says so explicitly since it's the heaviest card in the deck.
- Apostrophes in the text will need escaping (`\u2019`) to match the existing file style — parent agent's job.
